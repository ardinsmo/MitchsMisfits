How do I get involved?

Any Michigan Tech student is welcome to join The Misfits! Any student who sits in Section L at hockey games is a part of the group! By participating in the cheers you help us be the group we are today!

You can also sign up for our trips and attend our events when we have them! Be sure to check our Facebook and at games for more information.


As always, you can check the Michigan Tech Hockey website to find out when games are!
