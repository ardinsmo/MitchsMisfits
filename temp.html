Since 2004,

The Mitch's Misfits have been supporting Michigan Tech Huskies hockey at home in Section L of the John MacInnes Student Ice Arena and on the road across the United States.

We seek to provide a safe and fun way for students at Michigan Tech to enjoy Michigan Tech Hockey. Cheering on the hockey team happens not just at the rink, but also in the community. By providing trips and events to the community, we seek to extend the hockey experience beyond the rink!
